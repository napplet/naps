# NAAT-PET · `pet`

**Recommended open contract:** (none yet)
**Actions:** open

A napplet that presents and manages a virtual pet or companion.

- **IS:** a single companion surface for care, status, growth, play, or interaction.
- **IS NOT:** a user's identity (→ `profile`); a many-event list (→ `feed`); a publish composer (→ `composer`).
- **Distinct from:** `profile` (companion vs. user identity) · `feed` (one companion vs. many events) · `composer` (care vs. publishing).

> A NAAT file is this fixed schema and nothing else. Anything that wants to grow —
> payload detail, lifecycle, action-specific wire — belongs in conventions, not here.
> See [../ARCHETYPES.md](../ARCHETYPES.md) for the registry and [../naps/NAP-INTENT.md](../naps/NAP-INTENT.md) for how roles are opened.
